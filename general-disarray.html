General Disarray

About General Disarray

General Disarray from South Park is Professor Chaos’s overly loyal but dim-witted sidekick who enthusiastically supports his chaotic plans despite barely understanding them.

General Disarray and Professor Chaos

General Disarray’s Characteristics

  1. Loyal sidekick – he faithfully follows Professor Chaos’s lead.
  2. Low intelligence – he often misunderstands plans and situations.
  3. Eager enthusiasm – he is very excited to participate in "evil" schemes.
  4. Childlike personality – he behaves in a naive, immature, and playful way.

General Disarray’s Enemies

  • The Coon
  • Mysterion
  • Toolshed
