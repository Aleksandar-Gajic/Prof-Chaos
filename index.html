About Professor Chaos

Professor Chaos from South Park is a comically inept supervillain alter ego of Butters who dreams of spreading chaos but constantly fails in absurd and harmless ways.

Professor Chaos and his crew

Professor Chaos’s Characteristics

  1. Inept villainy – he tries to be evil but always fails in ridiculous ways.
  2. Overdramatic personality – he speaks and acts like a grand supervillain.
  3. Naive innocence – despite his "evil" plans, he remains childlike and harmless.
  4. Strong imagination – he fully commits to his fantasy of being a chaotic mastermind.
